Name, company, deal value, next steps — structured and pushed to your CRM the moment the meeting ends. Automatically.

Privacy by design

Audio never stored. Ever.

Glasscast transcribes in real time using your phone's native speech recognition. The moment text is extracted, the audio is gone. Only structured lead data — names, companies, values, actions — is transmitted to your CRM. Your conversations stay private.
